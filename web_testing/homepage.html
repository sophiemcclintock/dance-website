WELCOME

Based at Samuel Marsden Collegiate School, Riptide is a dance club who believe in dancing for the love of it. We offer a wide range of styles for all different abilities, whether you are a beginning or an advanced dancer, we would love to have you join our whanau. We are 100% devoted to encouraging a love of dance, whether it be through competitive achievements or purely for pleasure.
